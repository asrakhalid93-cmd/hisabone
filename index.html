About HisabOne

UAE tax & compliance for businesses and accountants — in one place, without the chaos of email and WhatsApp.

What we do

HisabOne brings UAE businesses and their accountants together on a single secure platform to handle FTA registrations, VAT and corporate tax filings, audit preparation, and document deadlines. Businesses keep every registration document, expiry date and request in one portal. Accountants manage all of their clients, request documents per process, and chase them automatically — so nothing is lost in an inbox and no deadline slips.

Our vision

We believe compliance should be simple, transparent and automated. Our vision is to remove manual follow-up and human error from tax and audit work across the UAE — using AI to prepare filings, track every deadline, and keep businesses and their accountants perfectly in sync, so a penalty is never a surprise.
