About HisabOne

UAE tax & compliance for businesses and accountants — in one place, without the chaos of email and WhatsApp.

What we do

HisabOne brings UAE businesses and their accountants together on a single secure platform to handle FTA registrations, VAT and corporate tax filings, audit preparation, and document deadlines. Businesses keep every registration document, expiry date and request in one portal. Accountants manage all of their clients, request documents per process, and chase them automatically — so nothing is lost in an inbox and no deadline slips.

Why it matters

Compliance in the UAE runs on chaos — scattered email and WhatsApp threads, manual spreadsheets, and follow-ups that depend on someone remembering. And non-compliance is expensive: a late VAT return costs AED 1,000–2,000, late-paid tax accrues 14% interest a year, and late corporate-tax registration carries a flat AED 10,000 penalty. With VAT since 2018, corporate tax since 2023 and mandatory e-invoicing arriving in 2026–27, every one of the UAE's 1.4 million registered businesses has to get compliance right — continuously.

Our vision

Make tax compliance automated, transparent and cost-effective for every UAE business and the accountants who manage them — contributing to the UAE's vision of AI integration across government and enterprise. Our mission is simple: remove manual follow-up and human error from tax and audit paperwork, so a penalty is never a surprise.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 July 2026

Who we are

HisabOne ("we", "us") provides a UAE tax & compliance workspace connecting businesses and their accountants, available at hisabone.ae. For any privacy question or request, contact us at info@hisabone.ae.

What we collect

When you create an account we collect your name or company name, email address and password (stored only as a secure cryptographic hash by our authentication provider). While you use HisabOne we store the compliance data you choose to put in it: document requests, deadlines, reminders, reports, and the company documents you upload to your vault.

How your data is used

Your data is used solely to provide the service — showing your own workspace, sharing records you explicitly share with your linked accountant or clients, and sending the emails you ask us to send (such as document requests and reminders). We do not sell personal data, and we do not use your data for advertising.

AI document reading

When you upload an invoice or company document for automatic reading, the file is processed by Anthropic's Claude API to extract the fields shown to you (amounts, dates, registration numbers). These files are processed to provide the feature and are not used to train AI models.

Where your data lives

Account and workspace data is stored with Supabase (managed PostgreSQL with row-level security) and our website is served by Vercel. All traffic is encrypted in transit using TLS. Access controls ensure each account can only read its own records and records explicitly shared with it.

Your rights

In line with UAE Federal Decree-Law No. 45 of 2021 on the Protection of Personal Data (PDPL), you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing us. Deleting your account removes your profile, workspace records and uploaded files from our systems.

Cookies & local storage

We use only the browser storage necessary to keep you signed in. We do not use advertising or third-party tracking cookies.

Changes

If this policy changes materially, we will note the new date at the top of this page. Questions? Email us.
